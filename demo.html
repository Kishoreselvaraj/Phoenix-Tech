Main Contant

The main content of the page is defined using the main tag, which can include various subsections represented by article and section tags. article encloses a block of content that makes sense on its own without the rest of the page, such as a blog post.

Article and Section

section is similar to article, but it is used for grouping together a single part of the page that constitutes one single piece of functionality, such as a map, or a set of headlines. It's considered best practice to begin each section with a heading. An article element can have different sections, and a section can include different articles. It all depends on the structure and content of your page. Knowledge is power

Aside

The aside element is used to define content that is not directly related to the main content but can provide additional information, such as related links, or similar articles.
